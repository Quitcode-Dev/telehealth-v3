# E-001: Patient Identity, Authentication & Account Management

**Priority:** must
**Status:** approved

## Description

Establish the secure foundation for all patient-facing interactions by implementing bilingual (Ukrainian/English) account registration, 2FA authentication via SMS OTP and Diia digital ID, and profile management. This epic covers personal account creation, demographic data capture, password/credential management, and GDPR-compliant consent collection. It serves as the prerequisite for all other portal features.

## Acceptance Criteria

• Patients can self-register a new account via web PWA in Ukrainian or English
• 2FA is enforced at login using SMS OTP; Diia digital ID authentication is supported as an alternative
• Patients can view and update personal demographics (name, address, phone, insurance details) without staff intervention
• Explicit GDPR and Ukrainian Law No. 2297-VI consent is captured, timestamped, and stored at registration and at each subsequent consent-required action
• Patients can manage communication preferences (push, SMS, Viber) from their profile
• Account recovery flow (forgotten password, lost 2FA) is available and secure
• All authentication events are audit-logged
• Platform is accessible in both Ukrainian and English languages across all account screens
• WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility compliance is met for all account management screens
