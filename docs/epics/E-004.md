# E-004: Insurance Verification, Cost Estimation & Digital Payments

**Priority:** must
**Status:** approved

## Description

Automate insurance verification at the point of booking and provide transparent pre-visit cost estimation showing total consultation price, insurance coverage, and patient co-pay before confirmation. Patients can pay co-pays and consultation fees digitally via LiqPay or monobank in-app, eliminating cash-only post-visit collection and reducing the 20% unpaid-visit follow-up burden on staff.

## Acceptance Criteria

• Insurance status (NHSU declaration or private/corporate policy) is automatically verified in the background during the booking flow
• Patients see a cost estimate — total price, insurance coverage amount, and co-pay — before confirming a booking
• Co-pay and consultation fees can be paid at booking via LiqPay or monobank in-app
• Payment confirmation is included in the booking confirmation notification
• Patients can view payment history and receipts within their portal account
• Admin panel shows insurance verification status and payment status per appointment
• Failed insurance verification triggers a clear patient-facing message and staff alert for manual resolution
• Payment processing meets PCI-DSS requirements and complies with Ukrainian financial regulations
• Digital payment option reduces post-visit unpaid bill follow-up cases measurably (tracked as a KPI proxy for operational efficiency)
