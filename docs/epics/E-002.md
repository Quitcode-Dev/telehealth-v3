# E-002: Online Appointment Scheduling & Automated Reminders

**Priority:** must
**Status:** approved

## Description

Deliver self-service appointment booking that replaces the telephone-only scheduling model, integrating with Helsi EHR via middleware to display real-time (2-minute cached) physician availability with conflict-locking. Patients can search by specialty or physician, view available slots, enter a reason for visit, confirm bookings, and self-serve reschedule or cancel. Automated multi-channel reminders (push, SMS, Viber) are sent at configurable intervals prior to each appointment to reduce the 34% no-show rate.

## Acceptance Criteria

• Patients can book an appointment in ≤4 taps/clicks on mobile from specialty selection to confirmation
• Real-time slot availability is retrieved from Helsi EHR via middleware with ≤2-minute cache; double-booking is prevented via conflict-locking
• Patients can filter by specialty, preferred physician, or 'any available', and by date range
• Patients can enter a free-text reason for visit (≤500 characters) at booking
• Booking confirmation is delivered via push notification, SMS, and/or Viber per patient preference within 60 seconds of confirmation
• Automated reminders are sent at 48 hours and 2 hours before the appointment (configurable per clinic)
• Patients can reschedule or cancel directly from the reminder link without needing to log in again
• Physician receives a pre-visit summary (reason for visit, current medications, recent labs, allergies) pulled from Helsi before the appointment
• Admin panel allows front-desk staff to view and manage the booking queue
• Booking availability respects clinic operating hours and physician schedule rules defined in Helsi
• System supports at least 65,000 registered users and peak concurrent booking sessions without degradation
