# E-007: Secure Patient-to-Care-Team Messaging

**Priority:** should
**Status:** approved

## Description

Enable asynchronous, HIPAA/GDPR-compliant direct messaging between patients and their care team within the portal, replacing unstructured phone calls for non-urgent enquiries. This epic covers the patient-facing messaging interface, care team inbox management, message routing rules, response SLA indicators, and admin escalation paths.

## Acceptance Criteria

• Patients can send non-urgent messages to their assigned care team directly from the portal
• Messages are routed to the correct care team shared inbox based on the patient's assigned clinic and physician
• Physicians and clinical staff can respond asynchronously from the Helsi-integrated physician interface
• Messages are end-to-end encrypted at rest and in transit
• Message threads are retained in the patient's portal history and care team inbox with full audit trail
• Patients receive a push/SMS/Viber notification when a care team member replies
• Response SLA indicator is displayed to the patient (e.g., 'Responses typically within 1 business day')
• Admin staff can escalate unresponded messages after a configurable time threshold
• Messaging system is not available for medical emergencies — a prominent disclaimer and emergency contact are displayed
• All message content is stored on Ukrainian-territory servers and complies with Ukrainian Law No. 2297-VI and GDPR
