# E-003: Lab Results Access & Notification

**Priority:** must
**Status:** approved

## Description

Enable patients to view their lab results digitally within the portal, replacing the current 2–5 business day phone/in-person delivery model with a target of under 2 hours from physician sign-off. The epic includes HL7 FHIR integration with Dila and Synevo laboratory systems (via Helsi), configurable physician-controlled release rules for routine vs. sensitive results, patient-friendly result presentation, and push notifications on result availability.

## Acceptance Criteria

• Lab results from Dila and Synevo are received via HL7 FHIR feed through Helsi EHR and surfaced in the patient portal
• Routine results (e.g., CBC, lipid panel, thyroid) are auto-released to the patient portal within 4 hours of physician sign-off
• Sensitive results (e.g., pathology, oncology, MRI findings) require explicit physician review and release before patient visibility
• Physicians can configure release rules by result category via their interface within Helsi (SSO access)
• Patients receive a push notification (and SMS/Viber per preference) immediately upon result release
• Results are presented in patient-friendly language with no unexplained medical jargon; reference ranges are displayed
• Physicians can attach a contextual note to a result prior to patient release
• Historical lab results are accessible within the patient's portal history
• All result access events are audit-logged with timestamp and user identity
• Lab result access time from physician sign-off to patient notification averages under 2 hours at 12-month post-GA
