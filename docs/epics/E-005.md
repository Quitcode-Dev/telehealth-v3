# E-005: Family Proxy & Dependent Account Management

**Priority:** must
**Status:** approved

## Description

Allow patients to manage healthcare for dependents (children and elderly relatives) under a single portal login with consent-based proxy relationships and a clear family account dashboard. This epic covers proxy account linking, consent and document upload workflows (birth certificates, power of attorney), profile switching, and aggregated reminder delivery for all managed family members.

## Acceptance Criteria

• A primary account holder can link and manage multiple dependent profiles (children and adults) from a single portal login
• Consent-based proxy relationship is established with a clear consent and document upload step (birth certificate for minors, power of attorney for adults)
• Proxy relationships are reviewed and approved by front-desk admin within a 24-hour SLA
• Full audit trail is maintained for all proxy access actions (booking, result viewing, profile edits)
• Profile switching between linked family members is available from the portal dashboard without re-authentication
• Appointment reminders are delivered for all managed family member appointments to the primary account holder's preferred channels
• Proxy access respects data minimisation principles — proxies see only data relevant to the dependent's care
• Document uploads (birth certificate, POA) are stored securely and are retrievable by compliance officers
• Proxy relationships can be revoked by either the primary holder or clinic admin at any time
• GDPR and Ukrainian Law No. 2297-VI consent requirements are met for each proxy relationship
