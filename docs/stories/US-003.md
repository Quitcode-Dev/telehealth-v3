# US-003: Automated Appointment Reminders

**Epic:** Not linked
**Priority:** must
**Effort Estimate:** M
**Status:** approved

## User Story

- **As a** patient
- **I want** to receive automated reminders before my upcoming appointment via my preferred communication channel
- **So that** I do not forget my appointment and can reschedule in advance if needed

## Acceptance Criteria

- Reminder is sent at 48 hours before the appointment and again at 2 hours before the appointment
- Patient can set their preferred reminder channel (push notification, SMS, or Viber) in their profile settings, with at least one channel mandatory
- Each reminder message includes the physician name, clinic address, appointment date and time, and a direct link to reschedule or cancel
- Rescheduling or cancelling via the reminder link completes the action within 3 steps without requiring the patient to navigate the full portal
- Cancellations or reschedules made via the reminder link are reflected in the Helsi EHR within 5 minutes
- If a patient cancels within 2 hours of the appointment, an admin alert is triggered in the front-desk admin panel
- Reminders are not sent for appointments that have already been cancelled
