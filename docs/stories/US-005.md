# US-005: Pre-Visit Patient Summary for Physicians

**Epic:** Not linked
**Priority:** must
**Effort Estimate:** L
**Status:** approved

## User Story

- **As a** physician (Dr. Melnyk)
- **I want** to view a pre-visit patient summary within Helsi before each scheduled consultation
- **So that** I can reduce non-clinical preparation time and walk into consultations with full patient context

## Acceptance Criteria

- Pre-visit summary is accessible to the physician via SSO from Helsi (SAML 2.0) with no additional login required
- Summary displays: patient-submitted reason for visit, current medications, known allergies, and most recent lab results — all pulled from Helsi EHR
- Summary is available to the physician at least 30 minutes before the scheduled appointment start time
- If the patient did not submit a reason for visit, the summary clearly indicates 'Not provided' rather than showing a blank field
- Physician can add a private pre-consultation note to the summary that is not visible to the patient
- Summary loads within 3 seconds of the physician opening it
- Accessing the summary is logged in the audit trail with physician ID, patient ID, and timestamp
