# US-002: Two-Factor Authentication at Login

**Epic:** Not linked
**Priority:** must
**Effort Estimate:** L
**Status:** approved

## User Story

- **As a** patient
- **I want** to log in to the portal using two-factor authentication via SMS OTP or Diia digital ID
- **So that** my health data is protected against unauthorised access

## Acceptance Criteria

- Patient can initiate login with a registered mobile number and receive a one-time passcode (OTP) via SMS within 30 seconds
- Patient can alternatively authenticate using Diia digital ID via the standard OAuth 2.0 / OpenID Connect flow
- OTP expires after 5 minutes if unused
- After 3 consecutive failed OTP attempts the account is temporarily locked for 15 minutes and the patient is notified via SMS
- A successful 2FA login redirects the patient to their portal dashboard within 3 seconds
- Session token expires after 30 minutes of inactivity and the patient is required to re-authenticate
- All authentication events (login, failed attempt, lockout) are written to a tamper-evident audit log
