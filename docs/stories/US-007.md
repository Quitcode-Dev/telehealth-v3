# US-007: Insurance Verification and Co-Pay Display at Booking

**Epic:** Not linked
**Priority:** should
**Effort Estimate:** L
**Status:** approved

## User Story

- **As a** patient
- **I want** to see my insurance coverage status and estimated co-pay amount before confirming an appointment
- **So that** I understand my out-of-pocket costs upfront and can make an informed decision before booking

## Acceptance Criteria

- During the booking flow, the system automatically checks the patient's NHSU declaration status or private/corporate insurance policy on record in Helsi
- The booking confirmation screen displays: total consultation price, insurance-covered amount, and patient co-pay — before the patient confirms
- If insurance verification cannot be completed within 10 seconds, the patient is shown an estimated price range with a message stating 'Insurance verification pending; final amount confirmed at check-in'
- Patient can proceed to confirm the booking whether or not insurance verification succeeded
- If a patient has no insurance on record, the full consultation price is displayed with a note to contact the clinic to add insurance details
- Co-pay amount matches the value computed by the Helsi billing rules engine with zero manual intervention required from front-desk staff
- Insurance verification result (covered / not covered / pending) is visible to the front-desk administrator in the admin panel booking queue
