# US-001: Online Appointment Booking

**Epic:** Not linked
**Priority:** must
**Effort Estimate:** XL
**Status:** approved

## User Story

- **As a** registered patient (Oksana)
- **I want** to book a clinic appointment online by selecting a specialty, physician, and available time slot
- **So that** I can schedule a visit without calling the clinic or waiting on hold

## Acceptance Criteria

- Patient can filter available slots by specialty, preferred physician name, and a date range of up to 30 days
- Available time slots refresh within 2 minutes of a slot being taken by another user, preventing double-booking
- Booking flow is completable in 4 or fewer taps/clicks on a mobile device
- Patient is prompted to enter a reason for visit (free-text, max 500 characters) before confirming
- A booking confirmation screen is displayed immediately after confirmation, showing clinic name, physician name, date, time, and appointment reference number
- Confirmation is also delivered via the patient's preferred channel (push notification, SMS, or Viber) within 60 seconds of booking
- Attempting to book a slot that has just been taken by another user returns a clear error message and prompts the patient to select an alternative slot
