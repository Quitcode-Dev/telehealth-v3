# US-004: Digital Lab Result Access

**Epic:** Not linked
**Priority:** must
**Effort Estimate:** L
**Status:** approved

## User Story

- **As a** patient (Oksana)
- **I want** to view my lab results in the portal as soon as they are available
- **So that** I receive my results within hours rather than waiting 2–5 business days

## Acceptance Criteria

- Routine results (e.g., CBC, lipid panel, thyroid) are automatically published to the patient portal within 4 hours of physician sign-off in Helsi
- Patient receives a push notification or SMS alert at the moment results are published
- Results are displayed with the test name, patient value, reference range, and unit of measurement
- Each result includes a plain-language interpretation note (no medical jargon), either auto-generated from a template or entered by the physician
- Sensitive result types (configurable by the physician/CMO) are held for physician review and not auto-released; the patient is notified that results are pending physician review
- Historical results are accessible and sortable by date and test type
- Lab result data is sourced from Helsi via HL7 FHIR and the source system name (Dila or Synevo) is displayed alongside each result
